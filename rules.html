Towers

Towers shoot farts at Mr. Sadaphal, they can be clicked on to upgrade.

To close the upgrade menu, please click on the tower specifically.

Towers can be placed on everything but the green path tiles.

They will grow and other stats will be improved as upgraded

Mixed Fractions

Mixed Fractions (when touching Sadaphal) either kill him or remove some of his health

Mixed Fractions can only be placed on the green path tiles.

Their price increases as you buy more.

Improper Squaring

The Improper Squaring tile slows down every Sadaphal on screen for 5 seconds.

Improper Squaring tiles can only be placed on the green path tiles.

Their price increases as you buy more.

Invisible Blocker

At the start of the map, from where the Sadaphals spawn, there is a blocker.

This prevents any towers or tiles to be placed to stop spawn killing, as it would make the levels TOO easy.
